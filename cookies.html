1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help improve your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for analytics (understanding visitor behavior) and affiliate tracking (attributing referrals to our site).

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our affiliate partners may place cookies when you click on referral links. We do not control these cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that this may affect site functionality.
